Group Courses

Suitable for both beginners and experienced users alike, our group courses are listed below:

Making Your Phone Your Friend

3 sessions, 1 hour per session, Sign up here

Serving as Tech Friend's most popular course, Making Your Phone Your Friend teaches you how to get comfortable with your smartphone. Jake Freeland, the instructor, starts at the basics: your smartphone's buttons, screen, and sound then guides you through getting apps and communicating with friends and family. At the end of this course, you will be able to use your phone with confidence and call your phone your friend.

Making Your Phone Your Friend Advanced

3 sessions, 1 hour per session, Sign up here

Making Your Phone Your Friend Advanced dives deeper into managing your photos and using social media. This class combines our Conquering Your Camera and Getting Social classes. If you're interested in taking either of those classes individually, we offer them below.

Conquering Your Camera

1 session, 1 hour, Sign up here

Conquering your Camera guides you through taking and managing photos on your phone. We will show you how to move photos from a physical album to your phone to look at any time. We will also discuss ways to backup your photos and keep them safe if your phone gets lost or damaged.

Getting Social

1 session, 1 hour, Sign up here

Getting Social introduces you to the benefits and dangers of social media. You will learn how to set up a Facebook account, search for friends, and see what's going on in their lives. We will walk through how Facebook works and discuss how you can share your experiences with friends and family using your phone.
